Moby Systems - Simple Location Sharing

Note: Pictured track was generated with testing data


The Moby Simple Location Sharing app was a startup that had the vision of making travel a social experience, allowing the user to share their journey online. The viewer recieves and draws real-time data, and uses the google maps api to chart one's trek to the grocery store or across the country. The user is able to upload photo and text pins to make note of remarkable events or their thoughts at the time. The timeline feature, to the right, allows an at-a-glance grouping of one's journey.
Unfortunately the startup is currently on ice, and not all of the features this concept has to offer made it to fruition.
